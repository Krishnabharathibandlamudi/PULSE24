About Us

Pulse24 ensures quick access to emergency healthcare by connecting users to nearby hospitals, specialists, and ambulance services. Our app also provides live consultations and an Emergency SOS feature to save lives in critical situations.

  • Nearby Care Finder: Locate hospitals and specialists instantly.
  • Live Consultation: Connect with top doctors anytime.
  • Emergency Assistance: SOS feature sends location for quick help.
  • Health Tips: Get expert medical advice for better well-being.
